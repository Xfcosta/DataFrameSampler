\section{Experiments}

The empirical scaffold evaluates DataFrameSampler as a practical example-data
generator rather than as a universal synthetic-data winner. The primary
measures are nearest-neighbour distance, real-versus-synthetic discrimination,
downstream utility lift, and distributional similarity.

Current comparisons include DataFrameSampler default and manual configurations
plus simple baselines: row bootstrap, independent columns, Gaussian-copula
empirical sampling, and target-stratified column sampling where a target exists.
