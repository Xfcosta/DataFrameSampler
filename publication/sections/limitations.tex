\section{Limitations}

DataFrameSampler has no formal privacy guarantee. It should not be presented as
differential privacy, formal de-identification, regulatory compliance, or
protection against linkage attacks.

The method has no global optimization objective. Per-column NCA blocks can
conflict, and the concatenated latent space is only an empirical construction.
High-cardinality categorical columns are used after a warning, so users should
preprocess identifiers deliberately.

Generation is a local linear transport heuristic. It can produce implausible
rows when the learned latent space is poorly conditioned, when categorical
decoders are uncertain, or when real-world constraints span multiple columns.
