\section{Conclusion}

DataFrameSampler provides a lightweight mixed-type dataframe sampler based
on iterative supervised NCA latent blocks and local mutual-neighbour transport.
The resulting estimator has a direct sklearn-style API and a file-oriented CLI,
but its modelling claim is intentionally narrow: it is a simple and inspectable
tool for example-data workflows, not a formally private or globally optimized
synthetic-data mechanism.

The mechanism and calibration diagnostics make this conclusion more precise.
Categorical NCA blocks are supported only where they improve held-out
categorical prediction against simple baselines, calibrated decoder
probabilities should still be interpreted through their measured diagnostic
quality, and manifold evidence should be read as an insertion-stress diagnostic
rather than as a preservation guarantee. Future work should therefore focus on
stability of the iterative latent construction, constraint-aware decoding,
stronger high-cardinality handling, and alternatives to independent per-column
decoders.
